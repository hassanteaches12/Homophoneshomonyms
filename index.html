Section B — Choose the Correct Word (Homophones)

Pick the correct word in brackets. These sound the same but have different spellings.

🥩
He doesn't eat much (meat / meet).
Answer: meat
🍪
Who (eight / ate) the last cookie?
Answer: ate
🏪
I need to stop (buy / by) the shop on my way home.
Answer: by
🦁
The lion has a beautiful, thick (mane / main).
Answer: mane
🐦
The little bird is afraid of the (pray / prey) bird that hunts it.
Answer: prey
🧺
Will you (dew / do) the laundry today?
Answer: do
